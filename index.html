About us

Situated within walking distance of South Tulum Beach in Tulum, Mamasan Treehouses & Cabins features a garden, free private parking, a terrace and a restaurant. The 4-star hotel also provides a bar and air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi and a private bathroom. The property also offers room service, concierge service and can organize tours for you.
All rooms at the hotel include a wardrobe safe, while some have a balcony and others have a garden view. In addition, accommodation at Mamasan Treehouses & Cabins provides towels and bed linen.
